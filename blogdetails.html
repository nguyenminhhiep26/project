Green Burger

In order to convey this newly launched product to all food lovers and foreigners coming to Japan, the JAPANKURU team immediately researched this burger. What's so special about this burger?

First, the meat of this hamburger is made from plant-based ingredients. It was from this period that there was a difference. The burger meat is completely meat-free, using only plant-based ingredients such as soybeans, konyaku, a plant-based jelly, and cabbage. This "meat" made from soybeans is filled with a unique umami flavor.

Green Burger is not only healthy, but also really delicious. So, next time you come to Japan, you should go to MOS Burger and order this dish.

Let's go to MOS Burger!

Green Burger is a brand new dish of MOS Burger, but MOS Burger also has other dishes that the team members love. More than half of the members of the Japankuru team love Mos Burger, the staple of the entire chain. A delicious piece of beef served with thick slices of succulent tomatoes, served with a special sauce. Truly a dish for those who love to eat meat

If you want a taste of history, you should order the teriyaki beef burger. This is Japan's first teriyaki burger and also created by MOS Burger! Other places have similar dishes but the taste is unmatched.

There is also a burger that is completely different from the burgers introduced before, which is a rice burger with fried oysters. Using rice as a bun for hamburgers, it's unique just looking at it. This is the creative thinking of MOS Burger that has been shown through teriyaki burger and Green Burger. And not only is this novelty, this rice burger is delicious too!
